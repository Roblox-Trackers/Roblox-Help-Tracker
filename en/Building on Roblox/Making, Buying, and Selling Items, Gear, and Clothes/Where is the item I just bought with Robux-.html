Where is the item I just bought with Robux?

If you're looking for your items you purchased from Avatar Shop , you can go to your Inventory to check your items. If you're looking for your Experiences items you purchased, you can go to Create to check your items. For more information, see I bought items from Experiences below.

If you're looking for items you purchased with your country's currency like Dollars, Euros, Pesos, Real, Yen, or Won, check out the Where is the item I just bought with money? article.

I bought items from Avatar Shop

Your Inventory has categories to help organize your items. Make sure to check all the categories such as hats, hair, animations, faces, and packages etc.

Instructions for viewing your Inventory:

  • Mobile App
  • Web Browser
  • Xbox One App

Other players can create Packages or Decals that look like official Roblox items, for use in decorating experiences. To tell the difference before buying, you can see what its item type is underneath its title on the item details page.

I bought items from Experiences

Roblox enables anyone to imagine and create immersive 3D experiences. You can buy perks and virtual items directly from these experience developers for Robux.

  • Experience passes are sold on the experience's Store page, and all other virtual items are sold inside the experience.
  • Pricing and quality are determined and maintained by the Developer and not by Roblox.
  • Try contacting the experience's developer through private messages or their groups.
