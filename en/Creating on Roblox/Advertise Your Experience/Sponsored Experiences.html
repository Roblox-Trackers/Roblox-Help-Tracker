Sponsored Experiences

You can use Sponsored Experiences to get your experience in front of more people. Sponsored Experiences are when a Roblox creator pays for their experience to appear in a special Sponsored sort in the Home page and Discover page on desktop, mobile, tablet, and consoles. Sponsored Experiences all appear within the dedicated Sponsored sort so players can tell them apart from other experiences they would typically see. You can learn how to create and use Sponsored Experiences in the Sponsored Experiences section here.
