Clothing: T-Shirts, Shirts and Pants

Roblox offers a multitude of ways to customize your avatars. One of these ways is by showing your personality with many of the different items offered in the Marketplace . While there are thousands of choices available, you can go even further with this customization by making your very own!


To learn more about how to create clothing, see the Classic Clothing article.

Note: All clothing must comply with the Roblox Community Standards .
