Clothing: T-Shirts, Shirts and Pants

Roblox offers a multitude of ways to customize your avatars. One of these ways is by showing your personality with many of the different items offered in the Avatar Shop . While there are thousands of choices available, you can go even further with this customization by making your very own! To learn about how to create clothing, see the Classic Clothing article.

Note: All clothing must comply with the Roblox Community Standards .

Classic T-Shirts

Classic T-Shirts are pictures that anyone can upload and display on their avatar. Since anyone can upload them, they are very abundant, just take a look through the Avatar Shop or create your own by clicking the Create a T-Shirt page.

Classic Shirts and Pants
