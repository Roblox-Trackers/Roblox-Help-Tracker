How Do I Get Promotional or Contest Items?

The Catalog has many different items that you can buy. But items won in a contest or received as gifts can be a special addition to your inventory along with the memories of receiving them.

How Can I Get Them?

These types of items are typically given in one of the following ways:

  • As a gift during holidays
  • Award for doing certain things during special events
  • Given as a Roblox Contest prize
  • A special promotion for purchasing a Roblox Gift Card

Note: On occasion, contest or promotional items may eventually go on sale, so be sure to keep an eye on the Catalog!

Where Can I Find Details About Awards and Prizes?

The rules for holiday/special event awards and contest prizes are made available in the following ways:

  • On our website via the official banner
  • The event's page
  • On our Official Roblox Blog

If all the conditions for a given item are not met, then it will not be given to the account.
