The Avatar Shop

The Avatar Shop is where you can go to buy most of the awesome clothes and items that you can use to customize your avatar. You can get there by clicking on Avatar Shop in the blue bar at the top of the site, or by clicking here .

The Layout

When you first enter the Avatar Shop, you will be presented with a list of new and Featured Items. This is a great place to see a lot of the hats and gear that everyone will be talking about. If you scroll to the bottom, you will be able to flip through various pages of items by using the supplied arrow buttons.

You'll also be able to search for items by keyword or title using the Search box at the top of the page. You can also browse by category using the menu on the left of the screen.

Narrowing Down Your Choices

Once you've selected the category or used the Search bar, you can narrow your search using filters such as Genre, Unavailable Items, Creators and Price .
