Audio Files

Roblox platforms provide creators and developers to upload audios to make their experiences immersive and dynamic. Adding audio to your experiences such as background music, narration, sound effects, and more!

Note: All audio uploaded to Roblox must meet our Roblox Community Standards .

How to Find Audio

Read the Finding Audio Assets article for information on how to find audio files.

How to Upload Audio

Uploading can be done through Studio or Create on a web browser. Follow the steps below.

Studio

Follow the steps in the Importing Custom Audio section.

Create

  1. Log in to your Roblox account.
  2. Click Create in the blue bar at the top of the screen.
  3. Click on Audio .
  4. Click Browse and select an audio file.
  5. Once selected, click on the "Upload" button.

How to Configure Audio

After the audio file has been uploaded, it can be further customized on the Configure page. Read the viewing permissions article for information on how to configure your audio files.

Note: Names and descriptions are subject to our text filters. For more information on these filters, read the Safety Features: Chat, Privacy & Filtering article.
