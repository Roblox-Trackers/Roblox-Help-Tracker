Selling Experience Access (Paid Access)

Paid Access allows you, the creator, to charge a one-time Robux fee in order for users to access an experience. Before you can enable paid access, your account must be at least 30 days old and your experience must be public to all users.

Read the Paid Access creator documentation for more information about Paid Access, how to enable it, and restrictions.


Note: Paid access experiences are not available on Xbox.
* Users cannot copy experiences with Paid Access, even if they were previously allowed to copy them.
