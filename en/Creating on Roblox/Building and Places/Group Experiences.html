Group Experiences

Group members with the correct permissions are able to create and edit experiences for groups which can be a source of revenue.

Group-Owned Experiences: Creating and Editing

Group experiences are experiences owned specifically by a group. Any funds earned from one of these experiences, Robux that are earned from sales or ads, will be applied to the group itself regardless of who created it. For more information on group revenue, read the article Group Clothing, Revenue Management, and Payouts .

Any member with the Create and Edit Group Experiences permission can participate in the building process for group places. To create a new group experience, please do the following:

  1. Click Create in the blue bar at the top of the site
  2. Click the Group Creations tab
  3. Select the group from the drop-down box at the top of the left navigation column
  4. Click the Create New Experience button
  5. Go through each tab and set up the experience as you wish. For assistance with configuring your experience, read the Publishing Experiences and Places article.
  6. Click the Create Place button

This will then create a private and empty place in your group's place list. To make the experience public and playable, click on Experiences in the navigation bar to the left and then click the grey Private button underneath the place's title. You can further edit or configure this or any other group owned experience by doing the following:

  1. Click Create in the blue bar at the top of the site
  2. Click the Group Creations tab
  3. Select the group from the drop-down box at the top of the left navigation column
  4. Find the place that you would like to edit or configure in the list
  5. To edit the place itself, click its Edit button. This will open it up in Studio so that you can make any needed adjustments
  6. To configure the place's basic settings, click the gear to the right of its Edit button and select Configure
