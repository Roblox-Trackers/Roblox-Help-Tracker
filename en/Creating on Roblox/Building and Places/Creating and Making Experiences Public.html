Creating and Making Experiences Public

You may find that developing one experience just isn't enough to satisfy your craving for creativity. Luckily, we can help! All users are able to build as many experiences as they want, though accounts are limited to 200 public experiences (able to be played by others) at any given time.

To Make an Experience Public or Private:

There are two methods of making an experience public or private.

Note: Making your experience private will shut down any running experiences and cancel all server subscriptions.

Method one:

      1. Click Create in the top bar of the website
      2. In the My Creations tab, click Experiences if it isn't already highlighted
      3. Find the place you would like to make public/private and click the grey or green icon underneath the place's title to switch between the two statuses
        • Public (green icon) will allow any user to view and play the experience
        • Private (grey icon) will prevent anyone other than you from being able to view or play the experience, unless it's a group experience, in which case only you and appointed developers (with the necessary rank with experience edit permissions) will be able to play the experience. For more information, please see the help article about Group Experiences .

Method two:

      1. Navigate to your experience's page
      2. Click the three dots icon to pull up the experience's settings menu
      3. Select Configure this Place or Configure this Experience
      4. Regardless of which you select in the above step, on the screen that appears, navigate to the Basic Settings tab if it isn't already highlighted
      5. Under Privacy , select either Public or Private
        • Public will allow any user to view and play the experience
        • Private will prevent anyone other than you from being able to view or play the experience, unless it's a group experience, in which case only you and appointed developers (with the necessary rank with experience edit permissions) will be able to play the experience. For more information, please see the help article about Group Experiences .

To Create a New Experience:

    1. Click Create in the top bar of the website
    2. In the My Creations tab click Experiences if it isn't already highlighted
    3. Click Create New Experience
    4. Choose the settings and templates for your new creation
    5. Click Create Experience

This will take you back to the Create page you were at previously. You can go ahead and make the experience public now, if you would like, or you can wait until you have done some work on it. To start building, click the Edit button to the right of your new experience's entry in your Experiences list.
