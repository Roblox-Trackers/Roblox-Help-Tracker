Disallow Copying of Your Experience

Disallowing copying of your experience prevents other people from directly copying everything you created. However, if you would like to allow others to copy your experience as a template for their own experiences, you can choose to allow copying.

There are two methods you can use to either allow or disallow copying.

Method one:

    1. Login and click on the Create tab
    2. Click on the gear-icon located to the right of your experience name. Then choose Configure Start Place in the menu that displays
    3. Click Permissions
    4. Under Other Permissions , if you wish to allow copying, you would check the box next to Allow Copying . If you do not wish to allow copying, you would leave the box unchecked.
      • Note: By checking this box, you are granting every other user of Roblox the right to use (in various ways) the content you are now sharing. If you do not want to grant this right, please do not check this box. For more information about sharing content, please review the Roblox Terms of Use .
    5. Click Save

Method two:

    1. Navigate to your experience's page
    2. Click the three dots icon to pull up the experience's settings menu
    3. Select Configure this Place
    4. On the screen that appears, navigate to the Permissions tab
    5. Under Other Permissions , if you wish to allow copying, you would check the box next to Allow Copying . If you do not wish to allow copying, you would leave the box unchecked.
      • Note: By checking this box, you are granting every other user of Roblox the right to use (in various ways) the content you are now sharing. If you do not want to grant this right, please do not check this box. For more information about sharing content, please review the Roblox Terms of Use .
    6. Click Save
