PlayStation App: General Troubleshooting

If you are experiencing issues with the Roblox App on PlayStation, we recommend going through the solutions provided by Sony on their PlayStation Support page: How to troubleshoot issues when starting a game

Note: Keep in mind we recommend these solutions for general issues and not just for situations where the app does not start.

If after going through the steps listed on that page, the issue(s) persist, contact Roblox Customer Support and provide the following information:

    • PlayStation Network account name:
    • Roblox Username:
    • Any Error Messages Received:
    • Any Error Code Received:

If there is any additional information, such as recent changes to either your PlayStation or Roblox accounts, or changes to your internet connection, list those as well along with any other details.

Note: PlayStation Network account names are case sensitive, so make sure that the spelling and format are correct when contacting us.
