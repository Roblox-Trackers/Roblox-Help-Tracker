What currencies does Roblox accept?

Roblox offers native pricing in some countries. If native pricing is not currently supported for your country, pricing will display in USD by default.

Most online payment methods will convert currencies automatically at the current exchange rate. Your banking institution or credit card company may charge a fee for this service. Check with your banking institution or credit card company for more information.
