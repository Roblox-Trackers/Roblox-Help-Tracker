Which payment methods can I use?

Each purchase must be paid in full with one payment method.

Browser App

  • All major credit and debit cards
  • Prepaid credit card
  • PayPal
  • Roblox Gift Cards

Mobile Apps

In-app purchases using:

  • Google Play
  • iTunes
  • Amazon

Xbox One App

  • Roblox Xbox One App, in-app purchasing
  • Xbox Live Website
