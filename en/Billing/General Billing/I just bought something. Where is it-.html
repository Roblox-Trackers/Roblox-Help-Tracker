How to find your products

  • Where are my Robux?
  • Where is my Membership?
  • Where is my virtual Catalog or in-experience item?

I still can't find my product

  • Be sure you're logged into the Roblox account where the purchase was made
  • If your account doesn't have the purchased product, follow the steps for your platform below. If you purchased with a credit, debit, or gift card, move ahead to the " I still need help " section.

Mobile Apps - iTunes, Android, Kindle, and Windows

  • Log out of your account
  • Force close the Roblox app
  • Reopen the Roblox app
  • Log into your account

Xbox One

  • Do not log out of your account
  • Close the Roblox app
  • Reopen the Roblox app

Browser App - Credit Card, PayPal, or Gift Card payments

Follow the instructions below to contact us.

I still need help

If your product still has not been applied, provide us with the following information on the contact form :

  • Roblox Username
    • If Xbox One App - also send Xbox Gamertag. Note: Gamertags are case sensitive.
  • Device where purchase was made
  • Date of Purchase
  • Product Purchased

Once you have received the email confirming the receipt of your message, reply with a screenshot or forwarded copy of your purchase receipt (.jpg or .png format only).

Important note: Jailbroken devices are not supported for use or purchasing products via the Roblox Mobile App
