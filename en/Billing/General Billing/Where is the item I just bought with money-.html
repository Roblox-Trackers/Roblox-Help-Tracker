I still can't find my Roblox products

  • Be sure you're logged into the Roblox account where the purchase was made.
  • If your account doesn't have the purchased products, follow the troubleshooting below.
  • If you purchased with a credit, debit, or gift card, see I still need help below.

Troubleshooting

Mobile Apps: iTunes, Android, Kindle, and Windows

  1. Log out of your account
  2. Force close the Roblox app
  3. Reopen the Roblox app
  4. Log into your account

Xbox One

  1. Do not log out of your account
  2. Close the Roblox app
  3. Reopen the Roblox app

Web Browser: Credit Card, PayPal, or Gift Card payments

  • Follow the I still need help instructions to contact us.

I still need help

If your product still has not been applied, provide us with the following additional information on the contact form .

  • Roblox Username
    • If Xbox One App: Also, send Xbox Gamertag. Note: Gamertags are case sensitive.
  • Device where purchase was made
  • Date of Purchase
  • Product Purchased

Once you have received the email confirming the receipt of your message, reply with a screenshot or forwarded copy of your purchase receipt (.jpg or .png format only).

Important note: Jailbroken devices are not supported for use or purchasing products via the Roblox Mobile App
