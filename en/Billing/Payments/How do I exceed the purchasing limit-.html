How do I exceed the purchasing limit?

It is not possible to lift the purchasing limits. They are in place to help enforce the safety and security for payments made on Roblox. Limits cannot be changed or altered by Customer Support.

If you are unable to complete a purchase due to our purchasing limits, you can instead buy a gift card that can be redeemed to make a new purchase. Please visit our gift card page for more information: https://www. roblox.com/giftcards-us
