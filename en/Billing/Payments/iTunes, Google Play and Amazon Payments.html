iTunes, Google Play and Amazon Payments

Robux and membership can be bought in the Roblox Mobile App found on the iTunes, Google Play, and Amazon app stores.

How to buy

  1. Open the App (Run program)
  2. Navigate to and then click on the (membership) or (Robux) icon
  3. Choose the membership or Robux amount you want to buy
  4. A pop-up window will appear, asking to confirm your purchase.
    To cancel the purchase before completing it, use the Cancel or Back feature of your device.


Note: R ecurring memberships purchased through Google Play or iTunes cannot be canceled by Roblox. These memberships are set up directly through your mobile app provider. Please see the Canceling a Mobile Membership for more information on how to cancel with your provider.

In-App purchases are billed to your iTunes, Amazon, or Google Play account. Keep a copy of your receipt for account ownership verification just in case you need it in the future.
