Xbox One App: Using Robux

Using Robux on Xbox

  • Buy new Packages to change the look of your Roblox avatar. These are located on the Avatar screen found by either selecting the Avatar image or by pressing the Right Bumper (RB).
  • Buy perks, upgrades, and items inside experiences on Roblox for Xbox. These will be displayed to you inside the experiences, with options and pricing set by developer of that specific experience.

Viewing Your Robux Balance

While on the Home screen, click the Right Bumper (RB) until you reach the Robux screen. On this page, in the lower-left corner, your current total is next to My Balance .
