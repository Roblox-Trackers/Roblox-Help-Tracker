Will my membership automatically renew?

Monthly memberships may be automatically recurring. How to check your membership status and expiration:

From the Roblox Mobile App

  1. Be logged into the account where the membership was bought
  2. Tap the three-dots icon in the lower right
  3. Tap Settings
  4. Tap Billing
  5. View your membership status and Expiration date or Renewal date

From a Browser

  1. Be logged into the account where the membership was bought
  2. Open the gear icon in the upper right
  3. Open Settings
  4. Open Billing
  5. View your membership status and Expiration date or Renewal date

Your membership will renew on the Renewal date and if it is not recurring or has been cancelled, it will expire on the Expiration date .

You can cancel on our website at any time before the renewal date. Your member benefits will continue until the expiration date.

If you want to avoid charges for the following billing cycle cancel before the membership renewal date.
