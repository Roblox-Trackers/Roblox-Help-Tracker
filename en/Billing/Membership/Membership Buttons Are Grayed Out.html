Membership Buttons Are Grayed Out

This means membership upgrading or downgrading is currently unavailable.

Reasons

  • Membership type does not allow for level change before it expires
  • Membership is currently renewing

Membership in Renewal Information

Possible Outcomes

  • Successful renewal - Membership continues and buttons will return to normal
  • Failure to renew - Membership expires and buttons will return to normal

There are several reasons a membership may not be able to renew, such as an expired or cancelled credit card or insufficient Gift Card credits.

The renewing process may take a few days so please be patient. If the process takes more than 10 days, contact Customer Support via the support form .
