Where are my Robux?

From the Roblox Mobile App

Robux Balance

  1. Be logged into the account where the Robux were bought
  2. Tap the Robux icon at the top of the app
  3. Your Robux balance is displayed at the top

From a Browser

Robux Balance

Your Robux balance is shown in the upper right of the browser window.

Robux Spending History

  1. Be logged into the account where the Robux were bought
  2. Tap the Robux icon in the upper right of the browser window
  3. Tap your Robux value
  4. This will load the My Transactions page.
    • View your Currency Purchases over the Past Day , Week , Month , Year.
    • View your Purchases to see how the Robux were spent.

From the Roblox Xbox One App

Robux Balance

  1. While on the Home screen, click the Right Bumper (RB) until you reach the Robux screen.
  2. On the Robux screen, in the lower-left corner, your current total is next to My Balance .
