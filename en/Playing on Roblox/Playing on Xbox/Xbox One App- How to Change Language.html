Xbox One App: How to Change Language

We have multiple translations available for official Roblox text in the Xbox Application. However, in-game content may still remain in English. We currently have these languages available:

  • Brazilian Portuguese
  • English
  • French
  • German
  • Italian
  • Russian
  • Spanish

How to Change

Change the language by going to Xbox System Settings > Change Language Settings .

Note: You may need to quit and relaunch the Roblox Application to see changes.
