How to Reset Avatar - In Experience

Reset Avatar allows you to reappear and start over at a spawn point. This is useful if your avatar loses an arm (specifically your weapon arm) or if you become stuck in a wall.

To reset your avatar

  1. Click on Roblox logo at the upper-left corner of the screen to open the menu.
  2. Click Reset Character or hit the R key on your keyboard.
  3. A pop-up window will appear, asking you to confirm the action. Click Reset and your avatar will reappear at a spawn point.
