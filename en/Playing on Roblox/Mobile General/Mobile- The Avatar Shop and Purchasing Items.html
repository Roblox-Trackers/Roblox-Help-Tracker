Mobile: The Avatar Shop and Purchasing Items

The Roblox Avatar Shop is available on the Roblox Mobile App and can be accessed through the navigation bar located at the bottom of the screen.

Searching the Avatar Shop

Tablets

  1. Tap the Avatar Shop button located at the bottom of the screen
  2. From here you can navigate the Avatar Shop just as you would on a desktop/laptop. For assistance with this, please click here

Phones

  1. Tap the More " ... " button at the bottom right corner of the screen
  2. Tap Avatar Shop
  3. Select the category you want to search ( Collectibles , Clothing , Gear , etc.)
  4. Scroll through the options. Once at the bottom, more will load automatically

Note: You can enter a keyword (ninja, pirate, etc.) in the Search bar to look for more specific items

Purchasing Items

Once you've made your selection, the Item page will display more information such as the name, description, and genre (if applicable). From here, you can make your purchase.

Tablets

The interface and purchasing process is the same as on a desktop/laptop computer. For assistance, please click here .

Phones

  1. Find the item you'd like to purchase in the Avatar Shop
  2. Tap the green Buy button.
  3. You will be taken to a confirmation screen. Tap Buy Now , then Continue Shopping .
Note: The price listed beneath the image for a Limited or Limited-U item will be the lowest price it is currently being sold at.
