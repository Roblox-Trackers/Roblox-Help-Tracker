Mobile: Searching and Browsing For Games

While you can search for games on Roblox through your computer, if you're using a tablet or a phone, you can also do it on the Mobile App version of Roblox.

How to Find Games

There are a couple of ways that searching for games on Roblox can be done.

Browse Categories

To do this, tap the Games icon located in the navigation bar at the bottom of the screen. From there, you can browse one of the default lists: Popular , Top Earning , Top Rated , Featured , and Recommended .

Note: You can also use the drop down boxes at the top of the screen to narrow the games down to a specific category or to pick a different list such as your favorites, friend activity, etc.

Use the Search Function

You can also search for games by title or keyword. Just do the following:

  1. Go to the Games section
  2. Tap the icon at the top of the screen.
  3. Enter a game's title or a keyword in the search bar.
  4. Hit Return or Search on your keyboard. This may look like an arrow pointing left or a magnifying glass.

Once you've found a game that you would like to play, tap its icon. This will load its page. Here you can read about the game, see leaderboards, etc. To play the game, tap the Play button.
