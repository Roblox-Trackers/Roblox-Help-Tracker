Mobile: Friend Requests

The ability to accept and send Friend Requests is also available on the Mobile App.

Accepting or Denying Friend Requests:

Phones:

  1. Tap the More button at the bottom of the screen
  2. Tap Friends (the current total of requests is displayed to the right)
  3. From here, tap Requests
  4. Tap either Accept or Decline

Tablets:

  1. Tap the Friends button on the bottom of the screen
  2. Tap the Requests Tab
  3. Tap either the Accept or Ignore button corresponding with the request that you wish to review

Sending Friend Requests:

Phones:

  1. Tap the More button at the bottom of the screen
  2. Tap Friends
  3. Tap the Search bar icon at the top
  4. Type in your friends username and search
  5. Click the add button on the right of their username

Note: If you have already sent a Friend Request, you will not be able to send another, and their profile will state "Pending" or it will show a "Loading" icon.

Tablets:

  1. Tap the Home button at the bottom of the screen
  2. Tap the icon at the top of the screen
  3. Type the name of the player you wish to befriend
  4. Tap on the player's name from the list
  5. Tap the Send Friend Request button

Note: If you have already sent a Friend Request, you will not be able to send another, and their profile will state "Friend Request Pending."
