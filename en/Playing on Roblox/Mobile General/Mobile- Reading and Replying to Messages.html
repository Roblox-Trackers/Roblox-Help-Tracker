Mobile: Reading and Replying to Messages

Your Roblox messages are available on the Roblox Mobile App and can be accessed with the Navigation bar at the bottom of the screen.

To Read Personal Messages

  1. Tap the " ... " icon at the bottom right corner of the screen
  2. Select Messages
  3. Scroll through your messages. Once at the bottom, more will load automatically.
  4. Select which message you wish to read.

To Reply to Personal Messages

  1. Tap the " ... " icon at the bottom right corner of the screen
  2. Select Messages
  3. Scroll through your messages. Once at the bottom, more will load automatically.
  4. Select which message you wish to send a reply.
  5. Tap Reply , enter your message, then tap Send .
