Mobile: Groups

The Roblox Mobile App allows players to stay updated on their groups and post on the group Wall.

Accessing Groups

To access groups through the Mobile App, do the following:

  1. Tap the More " ... " button at the bottom of the screen
  2. From here, tap Groups
  3. Select the group you wish to view

Note: You can also search for groups by entering a group name in the search bar, under the My Groups option located at the top right corner of the screen.

Interacting With Groups

Tablet

If you are using a tablet, you can interact with groups just as you would on a desktop/laptop computer. For further assistance with groups and group functions on tablets and computers, please click here .

Phone

If you are viewing the group page on a phone, you can view its Description, Members, and Wall, Store, Affiliates as well as perform the following (depending on group privileges):

  • Post on the wall - To do this, tap the box where it says "Say something...", enter your message and click Post .
  • Leave Your Group - To do this, tap the " ... " icon in the top right corner of the screen. Read the message in the pop-up window and tap Yes if you're sure you want to leave.
  • Make Primary - To do this, tap the " ... " icon in the top right corner of the screen. Tap on the "Make Primary" button from the drop-down menu.

Note: Use the Report Abuse button if there are wall-posts that violate the Roblox Community Standards .
