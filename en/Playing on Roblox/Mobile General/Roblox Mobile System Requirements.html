Roblox Mobile System Requirements

Current supported devices

Apple iOS

iPad Air or higher, iPad Mini 2 or higher, iPhone 5s or higher, and iPod touch 6th Generation. 64-bit iOS 9 or greater is required.

Android

The Roblox application supports Android OS 5.0 and higher .

Note: On June 16th, 2021, Roblox will no longer support Android 4.4. The new minimum version required will be Android 5.0.

Amazon Fire OS

Kindle Fire HDX 7 (3rd Gen), Kindle Fire HDX 8.9 (3rd Gen), Fire HDX 8.9 (4th Gen), Fire HD 6 & 7 (4th Gen), Fire HD 8 & 10 (5th Gen), and Fire (5th Gen) except: Amazon Fire HD 2013

Note: Newly released operating systems/devices or pre-released versions of operating systems/devices may not immediately be supported.

How do I check the current version of my device's operating system?

Apple iOS

    1. Navigate to the Settings app
    2. Navigate to General > About
    3. The iOS version is displayed in the Version field on this page

Android

    1. Navigate to the Settings app
    2. Scroll down to and select About phone
    3. Navigate to Software information
    4. The Android version is displayed in the Android version field

Amazon Fire OS

    1. Swipe down from the top of the screen to open Quick Settings
    2. Tap More
    3. Tap Device
    4. Tap About
    5. The Amazon Fire OS version is displayed on this page

Internet requirements

Roblox requires a minimum of a 4-8 Mb/s internet connection. If you experience problems and are currently connected to your phone company's 3G or 4G network, please try connecting through WiFi instead for a faster and more stable connection.

Android warning screens

Due to the very large number of Android devices in existence, we cannot be certain that all devices will run optimally even if they are running the correct version of the operating system and do not have a Tegra 2 chipset.

We have 2 levels of unsupported Android devices - skippable and unskippable:

  • Skippable Devices : These types of devices will get a warning telling you that your device may not be powerful enough to play experiences smoothly. This is just a warning to let you know that while your device isn't specifically supported, experiences might still run though they will likely lag a little (or a lot). Tapping outside the warning box will make it go away and the experience should keep loading.

  • Unskippable Devices : will get a message that informs you that your device doesn't meet the minimum system requirements. This means the device is not able to play experiences on Roblox. This message usually shows up on devices that use the Tegra 2 graphics chipset or a processor older than ARMv7. These kinds of tablets/phones do not support features that the Roblox needs to be able to function. However, users with these unskippable messages will still be able to access all other parts of the app outside of experiences.
