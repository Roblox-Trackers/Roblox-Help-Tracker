Mobile: How to Chat In Games

While you can chat with your friends on Roblox from your computer, you also have the ability to chat in-game on the Roblox Mobile App.

How to chat

If you would like to chat in-game, please do the following:

  1. Select a game. For information on how to search for games, please click here .
  2. To send your own messages, tap the icon at the top-left of the screen.
  3. Use the pop-up keyboard to enter your message, then tap its Return key . This may look like an arrow pointed to the left on the far right of the keyboard.

Note: You may also open and close the chat box at will by tapping the icon in the upper-left corner.

How to disable in-game chat

To disable the ability to chat in-game, perform the following steps:

  1. Log into the account
  2. Click the More " ... " icon located in the lower-right corner of the screen
  3. Select Settings
  4. In the pull-down menu, select Privacy
  5. Under "Who can chat with me?", select No one

Note: Please note that this chat will be subject to the account's privacy mode/filter. For more information on these filters, please click here .
