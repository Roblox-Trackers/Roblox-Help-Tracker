How to Follow or Join Another Player In Games

You can play with your friends in a game they're already playing! As long as your friend has the join in-game setting enabled, you will be able to join them in a game with a click of a button.

Note: You will only be able to join the game if you have the necessary permissions to join the game. For more information, please see our help articles Creating and Making Games Public and Group Games .

To Join Another Player

  • Search for the user's username or go to their Profile page
  • If that user is currently in a game and is allowing people to follow them, a Join Game option will appear on the search results or their profile page
  • Click Join Game and you will be placed into the game with them
