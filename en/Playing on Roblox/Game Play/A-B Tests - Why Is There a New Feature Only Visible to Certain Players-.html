A|B Tests - Why Is There a New Feature Only Visible to Certain Players?

At Roblox, we're always working to improve the experience. Part of this process is testing out new features on our site. As our team conducts these tests, some accounts may see different features at different times. If you're wondering when a new feature might be available to you, please keep an eye on our Blog for any official announcements.

Note: Your account is randomly placed in a test group. Your account's role in any test cannot be changed.
