Roblox Beta App

Roblox app alters the flow of how players join games and where they can purchase Premium and Robux.

How to Install

  • If your account has access to the Roblox Beta App, you will see a banner for the app.
  • Click on the Try It Now button in the banner.
  • A pop-up window will appear saying the Roblox Player is installing
  • A secondary pop-up window may appear asking you to select a program, select Open Roblox .

How to leave the Beta

If you want to opt-out of the Roblox Beta App, all you need to do is first uninstall it by following our How To Uninstall Roblox Help article. From there, you can click the play button on any game on our website to install our current version of the game.

Note: Just make sure not to click the "Try It Now" button on our beta banner if you don't want to go back into the Beta App.

Beta App Differences

While you may be familiar with most of the interface if you’ve been playing Roblox normally, there are some key differences to note while playing on the Beta version.


Main Menu

You’ll see that several features are directly available in the Beta App’s main menu, such as the Home Page, Games Page, Avatar Editor, and Chat Window. You may open any of these on the left panel when first opening the App.

Leave Game (L)

This button makes the player exit the game, making the player disconnect from the server they were on. After clicking the button, there is another message to confirm if they want to leave. Leaving games in this manner will not close the game window, and will instead return you to the main menu.

To fully exit immediately, you may click the top-right X button in the game window, or press the Alt + F4 keys.
