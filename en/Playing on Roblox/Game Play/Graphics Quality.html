Graphics Quality

Roblox provides the option to set Graphics QualityÂ automatically, with graphics controlled by the computer, or controlled manually by the player. To adjust the graphics setting, perform the following:

  1. While in a game, click on the menu icon, located in the upper-left of the screen.
  2. Select the Settings tab at the top
  3. To have your computer set Graphics Quality, change the Graphics Mode line to Automatic . Otherwise, change it to Manual and move the Graphics Quality slider to the right for better graphics, or to the left for better performance.
  4. Click Resume Game or hit ESC on your keyboard to return to your game.
