How to Record Videos

Sometimes you may want to save Roblox memory and a screenshot just isn't going to cut it. You need a video! To record a video, follow these steps:

  1. Go into the game, click the menu button in the upper-left corner
  2. In the game menu, click the Record tab
  3. To start recording click Record Video .
  4. Then do your thing! When you are done, click the red icon in the upper-left corner
  5. To view your video, click the My Videos link in the window that pops up

Note: Currently, the record feature is not available on Mac platforms.
