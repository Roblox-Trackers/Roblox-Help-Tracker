Meta Quest Open Beta FAQ

What is the Meta Quest Open Beta?

The Roblox app will be coming to Meta Quest, with an Open Beta on Meta’s App Lab. The Open Beta is an opportunity for our developers to gather feedback from VR users who join their experiences. Meta Quest headset owners can install the Roblox app and enjoy experiences together with Roblox users from Xbox, iOS, Android, and desktop devices.

Please note, the Open Beta will only support Meta Quest 2 and Quest Pro devices. Roblox on Meta Quest is available for any user 13 and older.

How do I access the Roblox Open Beta on Meta Quest?

You will need a Meta Quest headset (Meta Quest 2 or Pro).

*Note: Roblox does not support Meta Quest 1 headsets.

To install the Open Beta, you'll need to follow these steps:

  • Make sure your Meta Quest device is up to date. (Roblox requires v55 of Meta Quest software.)
  • Search for “Roblox” in the Quest Store and install it from there.
  • Start Roblox on your Quest device.
  • Sign in to your existing Roblox account using the Quick Log In option on your computer or phone.

*Note: At this time we do not support account creation or password sign in on Meta Quest devices.

How long will the Open Beta be available?

The Open Beta will continue until the public launch of Roblox on Meta Quest.

How do I log into my Roblox account during the Meta Quest Open Beta?

Sign in to your existing Roblox account using the Quick Log In option on your computer or phone.


*Note: At this time we do not support account creation or password sign in on Meta Quest devices.

What Roblox experiences are available on Meta Quest?

Any experience that has been published for VR users will be available on Meta Quest. There will be thousands of experiences that work well in VR. The Open Beta is an opportunity for our developer community to test their experiences for VR and decide if they want to publish their experiences for VR. We expect as the Open Beta continues, there will be more experiences available for VR.

Can I rate an experience from the Open Beta that I played with using a Meta Quest headset?

No, users will not be able to rate experiences from within Meta Quest headset. We would like to give the developers a chance to optimize their experiences for VR before getting rated.

Buying Additional Robux with a Meta Quest headset

Buying Robux will not be available in the Open Beta. Users can still purchase items, but they will not be able to buy additional Robux using their Meta Quest. We will support purchasing Robux while using a Meta Quest device soon.

Are there safety tools available in the Open Beta on Meta Quest?

Yes, Roblox users on Meta Quest have the ability to mute, block, and report abuse. See below for more details.

Muting another user’s voice chat

You can mute another user in a chat with voice experience by clicking the microphone icon that will appear over their avatar’s head or next to their name in the experience menu. Muting only applies to chat with voice and not text chat and will only last while you are in that experience session.

Blocking another user

You can block another user in an experience to prevent them from engaging with you in voice or text chat. Please see our help article here: How to Block Another User .

Report Abuse

If another user is violating the Terms of Use or Community Standards , use the Report Abuse feature to file a report with our moderation team. Please see our help article here: How to Report Rule Violations .

Safety Bubble

When the Safety Bubble is on, other avatars will fade and then become invisible to you if they get too close. The Safety Bubble does not affect the movement of other players.

For more information, please visit our Help FAQ on Safety.
