VR Menus and Settings

Understanding the menus and settings in VR can help you get the most out of your experience.

The menu splits into a bottom bar and side menu in VR.

The Bottom Bar follows your view and can be collapsed to the bottom of the screen.
It gives you access to the In-Game Menu, the Exit page and the Core UI toggles.
The In-Game Menu has additional options for VR comfort settings - Comfort, Default, and Expert modes as well as Custom where you may individually turn the following settings on and off:

VR Vignette Enabled
This toggles the blink and vignette comfort feature that reduces nausea from camera movement.

VR Stepped Rotation Enabled
This toggles between smooth and stepped camera rotation for 1st and 3rd person camera modes.

3rd Person Fixed Camera
In 3rd person mode, this toggles between a fixed camera and a follow camera.
