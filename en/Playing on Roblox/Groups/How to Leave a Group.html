How to Leave a Group

Sometimes you may feel like you just are not interested in being in a particular group anymore. If that is the case, you can leave it at any time.

How to leave a group

  • Go to your Groups section
  • Click on the group you would like to leave
  • Click the Leave Group button on the side right of the page like below

Group ownership

If the owner leaves the group, then any group member with a membership can claim ownership. If there are no other group members with a membership at that time, then the group will remain without an owner.
