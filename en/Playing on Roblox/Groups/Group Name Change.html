How to change a Group Name?

  1. Navigate to the group whose name you'd like to change.
  2. Click the three dots menu to the right of the group name and click Configure Group .
  3. Navigate to the Information section.
  4. Click on the edit button to the right of the group name.
    • Enter the new group name.
    • Accept the terms and conditions by clicking on the Checkbox.
    • If you do not want to change the name, click Cancel or the X in the upper right.
  5. Click on icon to change the Group Name.

Past Group Name

Via the app

View by tapping the Previous Group Names text on the group details page or under the current name in search results.

Frequently Asked Questions

Question: How often can a group owner change the group name?

Answer: There is a 90 day period required between group name changes.

Question: My new Group Name got moderated can I change it before 90 days?

Answer: 90 days restriction doesn't apply if the new name of the Group gets moderated. Users can change it by following the above steps.

Question: How will group members be notified of the new group name?

Answer: Each member of the group will receive a message informing them of the name change.
