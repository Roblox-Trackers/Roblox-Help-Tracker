Social Links on Group Descriptions

Group administrators will be able to include social links within their group descriptions, similar to the method used in their user profiles.

These are the social links that can be added:

  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Youtube
  • Twitch
  • Discord

Adding social links to the group description:

    1. Log into your account
    2. Go the Group page
    3. Select the group in which you want to add the links
    4. Click the three dots menu on the right of the page and click ConfigureÂ Group button
    5. Select the Social Links option and click on Add Link
    6. Enter the URL, select the media type, and enter the title


    7. Click Save
    8. Once you save the links, this is how the social links will look on the group page.

Important Notes :

  • The social links will be visible only to accounts that are above 13. They will not be visible to under 13 accounts.
  • Although Discord links will be allowed within the social links feature, users are not allowed to discuss or share discord links anywhere else and may be moderated if mentioned in other place.
