PlayStation App: How to Play a Roblox Experience

A PlayStation console is required to play Roblox experiences, as well as an internet connection. You can find Roblox in the PlayStation Store. It's free to play!

There are many different experiences you can play on Roblox. When you first start out, you can find them on the Home screen and Experiences screen. After you've played some experiences, you'll also find your recently played experiences on the Home screen.

Each Roblox experience will have slightly different rules, controls and mechanics. The general controls will be the same, as shown below.

Note: This controller map can be accessed at any time in-experience by clicking the ‘Options’ button on the PlayStation controller to open the Roblox menu. Press the right bumper (R1) to view the ‘Help’ tab. Press the left bumper (L1) to view other tabs.

With the launch of any new platform, such as PlayStation, users may notice buttons for other consoles/platforms on PlayStation as Roblox developers update their experiences to include the PlayStation button mapping, user interface updating, or others .
