Find Friends Feature

Our Find Friends feature allows you to import your contacts from a mobile device and add them as friends on Roblox if they have a Roblox account. It also allows you to invite friends to join Roblox if they do not already have an account.

Note: No user on Roblox will be able to see your phone number. Roblox does not share your phone number or contacts with any third parties.

Preliminary Requirements

  • Must reside in the US
  • Must be 13 years or older
  • Must have a mobile device with Android OS 7.0 or newer, or iOS 9.0 or newer. If your device OS is older, we encourage you to upgrade it to use this cool new feature.

Unfortunately, we are unable to make this feature available to all users. We hope to be able to provide this feature to more users gradually and will announce when it is available to new segments of users. Stay tuned!

Add Friends

    1. Navigate to the friends list page on the Roblox mobile app. (For more information, please see our Android or iOS help articles)
    2. Tap the Find Friends button
    3. If a permissions dialog appears, grant Roblox permission to access your contacts so we can find your friends
    4. If a contact has linked their phone number to their Roblox account, tap the Add button next to their name to send a friend request

Invite Friends

    1. Navigate to the friends list page on the Roblox mobile app. (For more information, please see our Android or iOS help articles)
    2. Tap the Find Friends button
    3. If a permissions dialog appears, grant Roblox permission to access your contacts so we can find your friends
    4. If a contact has not linked their phone number to their Roblox account, tap the Invite button to customize an SMS message inviting them to sign up on Roblox

You can also filter your imported contacts list by searching for their first or last name.

Opt-out of Phone Number Discovery

    1. Navigate to your Settings page. (For more information, please see our Android or iOS help articles)
    2. Select Privacy settings
    3. Toggle the option Who can find me by my phone number? to No one

Remove your Contacts from Roblox

You can go to your device's privacy or app settings to turn off Contact access for Roblox. For further assistance with this, please refer to your phone manufacturer's help documentation and support services.
