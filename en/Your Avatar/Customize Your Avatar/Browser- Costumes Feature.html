Browser: Costumes Feature

Roblox not only allows users to change their avatar in fun and unique ways, but also the ability to save combinations for quick changing. The Costumes feature allows a user to save many different versions of their avatar. To use this feature, do the following:

  1. Navigate to your Avatar section located in the navigation menu
  2. Add or remove items until your avatar has a look you like
  3. Once the avatar is set, tap the Costumes tab
  4. Tap Create New Costume
  5. Enter a name and then tap Create

Once a new costume is created, the following options become available:

  • Update
  • Rename
  • Delete

Tap the gear-icon, located at the lower-right of the outfit, to bring up the options.  A confirmation pop-up window will come up when you select an option.

Note: You can create up to 50 custom costumes on your account. When updating a current costumes, the action will overwrite the costume with the avatar's current appearance.
