Mobile: Customizing Your Avatar

Check out the Mobile: Viewing Your Inventory help article for how to access your inventory on mobile.

How to wear or remove an inventory item

Phone

  1. Select the item you wish to wear or remove from your inventory
  2. To wear the item, move the Wear This slide bar to Yes
  3. To remove the item, move the Wear This slide bar to No

Tablet

  1. Tap the More button at the bottom of the screen
  2. Tap Avatar
  3. From here you can dress your avatar as you would through the website. For assistance with this, please tap here .

How to change your avatar's skin color

Phone

It is currently not possible to change your skin color using the mobile app on a phone, however, this can still be done through the web browser by accessing the full site.

  1. Open your device's web browser
  2. Navigate to roblox.com
  3. If you aren't logged in, tap View Full Site , log in, and skip to step 5. If you are logged in move to step 4.
  4. If you are logged in, tap the icon that looks like three stacked lines in the upper-left corner to bring up the menu, scroll to the bottom, and tap Full Site
  5. Once the full site has loaded tap the icon that looks like three stacked lines in the upper-left to pull up the menu (if it isn't already open)
  6. Tap Avatar
  7. From here you can change your skin color as you would on a computer. For assistance with this, please tap here .

Tablet

  1. Tap the More button at the bottom of the screen
  2. Tap Avatar
  3. From here you can change your skin color as you would through the website. For assistance with this, please tap here .
