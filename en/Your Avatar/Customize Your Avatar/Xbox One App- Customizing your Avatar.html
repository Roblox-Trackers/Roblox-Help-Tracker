Xbox One App: Customizing your Avatar

You can change the look of your Avatar by going to the Avatar tab and selecting the one that appeals to you. There are several free Avatars and many for sale for Robux.

You can furtherÂ customize your Xbox avatar via the Avatar Editor. To access it:

  1. Log into your Roblox account via the Xbox app
  2. Navigate to the Avatar tab
  3. Press the Y button

On this screen, you can edit various aspects of your avatar's look, such as Clothing, Body, Animation and Outfits. You can also toggle your avatar between R6 and R15 and review your look in Full View .
