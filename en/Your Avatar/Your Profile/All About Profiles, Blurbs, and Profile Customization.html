All About Profiles, Blurbs, and Profile Customization

Viewing a profile

To view your own profile, simply tap your own username on the Home screen or tap Profile from the navigation menu. Viewing someone else's profile:

  • Using the Search Box: You can type the name of the profile you wish to view into the search box at the top of the app.
  • Item or Experience Creators: You can view the profile of an experience or item's creator by going to its details page and tapping on their name. For experiences, this is located beneath the title and for items this is located to the right of its image.

The basic info box

The first thing you see when viewing a profile is the basic information box. Here you will find the user's player icon, user name, their most recent status update, and interaction options.

The Creations tab

You can view the items or experiences that a player has created by tapping on the Creations tab of the player profile. This is located beneath the basic information box. This tab will display everything the user has created separated by category.

By default, the experiences will display in a detailed view that focuses on one experience at a time and can be scrolled through by tapping the arrow icons on the left and right of the description box. You can, however, display multiple experiences at once by tapping on the icon.

Choosing what shows on your Creations tab

Occasionally you may find that some of your creations are better examples of your building abilities or personality than others. If this is the case, you can choose to only showcase specific ones. To do this:

  1. Visit the experience/item's details page
  2. Tap the gear-icon or icon that looks like three dots to the right of the title
  3. In the menu that pops up tap either Add to Profile or Remove from Profile

The About tab - the Blurb and Social Networks

Occasionally users may wish to let people know what they're all about, or maybe they just want to entertain their fellow players with some funny quotes. The blurb, visible in a profile's About tab, is the place to do this. To create one of your own or edit your already existing one:

  1. Log into your account
  2. Go to the Profile page
  3. Type your new blurb into the About box
  4. Click Save

How to add Social Links to an account

If you are 13 or older, you can add social networks to your own profile from within the Social tab of your Account Settings page by following the steps below:

  1. Log into your account
  2. Tap the icon that looks like a gear located at the upper-right of the page
  3. In the pop-up menu, select Settings
  4. Scroll down to Social Networks
  5. Enter the URL, tap Save

  6. Select the Visible to option for whom you want to show these links.

Note: Social media links will be visible only to accounts that are above 13.

In the upper-right of your blurb box, you will see any social networks that a player has linked.

The About tab - Currently Wearing

In the Currently Wearing section of a profile's About tab, you'll see a picture of how the player's avatar currently looks. You can toggle between 2D and 3D views by tapping on the corresponding button in the image's upper-right corner. In the box to the right of this picture, you'll see a list of everything the user is wearing. If you see something you like, you can tap on it to buy one for yourself or get more details.

The About tab - Other Sections

Below are the other sections you'll find in the About Tab:

Friends

Lists up to nine of the player's friends. If they have more than nine, you can view all of them by tapping the See All button.

Collections

This will show you up to six of the items a player has purchased. By default these will be the most recently obtained items, but the player can choose what displays in this section if they would like. To view a player's entire inventory divided by category, tap on the Inventory button. We shall discuss how to choose what goes in this section a bit later in this article.

Groups

This section will show all of the groups the player belongs in. Much like the experiences section of the Creations Tab, the groups will display in a detailed view that focuses on one at a time. You can scroll through them by tapping the arrow icons on the left and right of the description box. You can, however, display multiple groups at once by tap on the icon.

Favorite Experiences

Here you will see a selection of experiences that a player has favorited. To view all of a player's favorites, simply tap the Favorites button. For information on how to favorite a experience yourself, tap here

Roblox Badges

This shows a list of all of the Roblox created badges that the user has earned. To see how a badge was obtained, simply tap on it.

Player Badges

This lists the player-created badges that a player has obtained by doing various things in specific experiences. For more information on how a badge is earned, simply tap on it.

About tab - Choosing What Displays in Your Collections

Sometimes you may wish to show specific items in your profile's Collections bar. To do this:

  1. Visit the details page for an item you own
  2. Tap the gear-icon to the right of the title
  3. In the menu that pops up tap either Add to Profile or Remove from Profile
