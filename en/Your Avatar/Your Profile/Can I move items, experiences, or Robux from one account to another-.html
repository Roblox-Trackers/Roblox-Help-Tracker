Can I move items, experiences, or Robux from one account to another?

There is no system in place to transfer items or Robux between your accounts. It is possible to transfer your experiences using Roblox Studio.

To move a place between accounts:

  1. Run Roblox Studio.
  2. Log into the account with the place you wish to move.
  3. Go to File, then click Save As . Enter a file name.
  4. Log out of the account.
  5. Log into the account you want to put the place on.
  6. Go to File, then click Open . Â Select the place.
  7. In the place tab, go to File, then click Publish to Roblox .
  8. Select which experience to replace or select New Experience .
  9. If selecting New Experience , enter in-experience settings, then click Finish .
