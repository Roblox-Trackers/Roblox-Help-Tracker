Can I get an Administrator badge?

Administrator badges are given out to people who work for Roblox to make them easily identifiable to the community. Administrators and Moderators are all adults over the age of 18 who work at Roblox as a paying job.

If someone tells you they are an Admin or a Mod, but they don't have the badge, please be sure to report them right away using the Report Abuse buttons located around the site and in every game menu.
