I Forgot My Username

This article will walk you through the steps to find your Roblox username if you had a verified email address or phone number.

Find username using your email address

  1. Select Forgot Username or Password on the Login page.
  2. The page should default to the Forgot My Password tab, you will need to select the Username tab.
  3. Enter your email address in the Email box and press the Submit button. You will see a confirmation message.

    Please make sure to type your email address exactly as you entered it on your account.
  4. This will send an email with a list of all your accounts.
  5. After retrieving your username you can log in.

If, after a few minutes, the email doesn't seem to arrive, please be sure to check your junk/spam folders .

Find username using a phone number

  1. Select Forgot Username or Password on the Login page.
  2. Select Use phone number to retrieve username .
  3. Select your country code.
  4. Enter your phone number in the Phone Number box and press the Submit button.
  5. This will send an SMS with your usernames to your phone.
  6. After retrieving your username you can log in.

Other ways

These methods will only work if there was an email or phone number added to the account before you forgot the username. The only other way to locate the username is if you made a purchase on Roblox. Some purchase receipts include the username.

Sadly, without an email, phone number, or purchase history there is no way to recover the account.
