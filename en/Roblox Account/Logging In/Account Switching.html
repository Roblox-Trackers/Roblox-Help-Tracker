Account Switching

What Is The Account Switcher?

The Account Switcher enables Roblox users with multiple accounts to easily access and switch between these accounts on the web.

How Do I Access The Account Switcher?

Click on the Gear icon at top right of the Home page , then select Switch Account .

Note : This feature only works for accounts over 13 years old and accounts without parent privileges.

How many accounts can I log into with the Account Switcher?

You can add up to five accounts using the Account Switcher.

Will accounts added to the Account Switcher show up on other devices or visible to other people?

No. Accounts on the account switcher are completely private and only stored on your device. It does not show other users which accounts you are switching between and won’t show up on your other devices.

I have multiple accounts. If I violate Roblox’s Terms of Use, and the Trust and Safety team decides to take action on my account, will they take action on more than one of my accounts?

To keep our community safe, if a user violates our Community Standards, we may take action on different accounts that we determine belong to the same user. There are several factors we consider when making that determination.
