How do I delete my account?

Based on applicable law, you can request we delete personal information we collect or maintain about you, subject to certain exceptions prescribed by law.

To make a deletion request under applicable law, please contact us by using our support form. We will process such requests in accordance with applicable laws. To protect your privacy, we will take steps to verify your identity before fulfilling your request.
