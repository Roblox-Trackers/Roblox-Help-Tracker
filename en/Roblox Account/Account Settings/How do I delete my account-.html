How do I delete my account?

You can request we delete personal information we collect or maintain about you. To make a deletion request, please contact us by using our support form and select the desired Right To Be Forgotten option under the Data Privacy Requests . To protect your privacy, we will take steps to verify your identity before fulfilling your request.

Roblox is constantly evolving to bring an even better experience to all of our players and we hope to see you back!
