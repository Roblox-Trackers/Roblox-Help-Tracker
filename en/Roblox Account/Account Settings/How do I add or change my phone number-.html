How do I add or change my phone number?

This feature is for players who are 13 or older. A phone number may only be used on one Roblox account.

Adding a Phone Number

  1. Log into your account
    (If you can't log in, try these steps for recovering your password .)
  2. Go to account Settings
    • Browser - find t he gear-icon located at the upper-right corner of the site
    • Mobile Apps - find the three dots icon for More
  3. Select the Account Info tab
  4. Select the Add Phone button
    1. Select your location from the pull-down menu
    2. Enter the phone number and account password
    3. Select Add Phone

Changing or Removing a Phone Number

  1. Log into your account
    (If you can't log in, try these steps for recovering your password .)
  2. Go to account Settings
    • Browser - find t he gear-icon located at the upper-right corner of the site
    • Mobile Apps - find the three dots icon for More
  3. Select the Account Info tab
  4. Select the Update Phone button (icon looks like a pencil and paper)
    1. Select your location from the pull-down menu
    2. Enter the phone number and account password
    3. Select Edit Phone or Remove Phone Number

Commands for Text Messages

Here are some useful commands to start and stop text messages you may receive from Roblox:

  • STOP, STOPALL, UNSUBSCRIBE, CANCEL, END, and QUIT
    These single-word commands will stop future text messages. Note only single-word messages will trigger the block. For example, replying “STOP” will ensure you receive no further messages, but replying “STOP PLEASE” will not.
  • START, YES, and UNSTOP
    These commands will allow you to opt back in to receiving text messages.
  • HELP and INFO
    These commands will provide information you can use the above commands to control the delivery of messages.
