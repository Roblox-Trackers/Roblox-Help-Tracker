Changing Your Display Name

Roblox now allows all users worldwide to change their Roblox account's display name in the account settings as a free feature. This is different than Changing a Username .

How do I change the display name?

  1. Log into your account
    (If you can't log in, try these steps for resetting your password )
  2. Go to account Settings
    • Browser - find the gear-icon located at the upper-right corner of the site
    • Mobile Apps - find the three dots icon for More
  3. Select Account Info
  4. Select the Change Display Name button (icon looks like a pencil and paper)
    • Enter your new Display Name
  5. Click Save
  6. When you refresh you should now see your Display Name as different from your Username.

Note : Some users will see their display name referred to as "Name".

What else should I know about display name changes?

  • Your display name and username will be visible on Roblox and associated experiences
  • You can only change your display name one time every seven days.
  • Display names must follow Roblox Community Rules and terms of use or may be reset to your Username.
  • Other players may also have the same display name.
