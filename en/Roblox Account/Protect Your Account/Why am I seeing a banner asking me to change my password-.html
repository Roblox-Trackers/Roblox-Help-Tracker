Why am I seeing a banner asking me to change my password?

If there is suspicious activity in your Roblox account or we detect that your password might be stolen from other services*, we may ask you to change your password. By changing your password, you help make sure that only you can use your account.

Help secure your account immediately

  1. Sign into your account.
  2. You will be asked to change your password. Click Change Password on the banner and you will be prompted to enter your current and new password.
  3. Create a new strong password that you don’t use for any other services and is different from ones you have used before.
  4. Consider using a password manager to generate strong and unique passwords, so that you don’t have to remember it.

Secure your account more with 2-step verification

2-Step verification makes it so that no one else can login to the account even if they know the password. When you log in from a new device, Roblox will send you a code, and you can only log in if you have that code. Check out the Add 2-Step Verification to Your Account help article for more information.

  1. Turn on 2-step verification via an authenticator app
  2. Turn on 2-step verification via email

*Your password might be stolen from other services through third-party data breaches, if you use the same password across different websites. To protect your account, create a unique password only for Roblox.
