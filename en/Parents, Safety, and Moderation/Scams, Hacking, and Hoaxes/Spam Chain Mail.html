Spam Chain Mail

If you receive a message that says you have to forward it or something bad will happen, don't worry as it is just spam and isn't true. You do not have to forward it.

You can just ignore these messages or report them to our team for spamming with the Report Abuse feature. Please do not forward them as your account could also get a warning for spamming. No one likes this kind of spam!
