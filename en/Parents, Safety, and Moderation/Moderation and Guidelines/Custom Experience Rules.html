Custom Experience Rules

What do I need to know about custom rules?

The following are important to keep in mind with custom experience rules:

  • Such rules are allowed as long as they don't conflict with the Roblox Community Standards
  • It is optional for developers to make these rules
  • Breaking these rules is not able to be reported with our Report Abuse system
  • The developer must make their own systems to handle the breaking of their custom rules

Please be aware that excessively mean-spirited rules can fall into the realm of harassment, which is not allowed on Roblox. In such situations, please utilize our Report Abuse and Blocking features.
