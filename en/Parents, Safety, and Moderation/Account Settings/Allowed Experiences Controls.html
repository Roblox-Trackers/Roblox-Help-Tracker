Allowed Experiences Controls

Roblox Experience Guidelines, which provide age recommendations for the content suitability of an experience. Based on these age recommendations, users and parents can make informed decisions about the content they interact with. Parents can use Allowed Experiences to manage access to the content their child has access to. Parents can learn more about our parental controls in our Parents Guide .

What happens when Allowed Experiences are set?

Parents are able to select age-appropriate experiences by enabling Allowed Experiences. This will allow your child to only join experiences that match or are below the age recommendations set. Experiences above the age restriction enabled may be displayed in search results or advertised in the ads. However, the experiences will not be viewable. Users will receive a message stating the experience is unavailable due to the account restrictions settings.

How do I configure Allowed Experiences?

On a Computer

  • Log into the account (If you cannot access the account, please click here )
  • Click the gear-icon located at the upper-right corner of the site
  • Select the Settings option indicated by the gear icon
  • You must have a Parental PIN set up to configure this feature
    • Here are steps for configuring Parental PIN
  • Select Parental Controls option
  • In the Allowed Experiences section, select the highest level of experiences you want the account to be able to join
  • Once selected, a green message will be displayed "Saved Successfully!"

Please note: If the account has enabled Account Restrictions, the account will be restricted to All Ages content only and disable Allowed Experiences. The Account Restrictions setting needs to be disabled before Allowed Experiences can be adjusted. For more information, please read Account Restrictions help article .

On a Mobile Device

  • Log into the account in the app (If you cannot access the account, please click here )
  • Click the More button indicated with three dots located at the lower-right corner of the page
  • In the pop-up menu, select the Settings option
  • You must have a Parental PIN set up to configure this feature
    • Here are steps for configuring Parental PIN
  • Select Parental Controls in the menu on the left-hand side of the screen
  • In the Allowed Experiences section, select the highest level of experiences you want the account to be able to join
  • Once selected, a green message will be displayed "Saved Successfully!"

Please note: If the account has enabled Account Restrictions, the account will be restricted to All Ages content only and disable Allowed Experiences. The Account Restrictions setting needs to be disabled before Allowed Experiences can be adjusted. For more information, please read the Account Restrictions help article .
