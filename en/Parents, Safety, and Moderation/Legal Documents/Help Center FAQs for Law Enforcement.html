What is Roblox?

Roblox is a user-generated content (UGC) platform where people of all ages can engage in a variety of ways, both socially and through creation and play. Roblox also provides a variety of age-appropriate settings and parental controls for chat and content accessibility. How Can Law Enforcement Contact Roblox?

Roblox sometimes receives requests from law enforcement seeking information about users involved in a legal proceeding. If you are a law enforcement agent aiming to serve Roblox with a subpoena, search warrant, preservation request, or similar legal process, please submit your request via our portal here: Roblox Law Enforcement Portal .

Please note that all legal processes should comply with applicable law such as 18 USC 2701-2712 . Roblox will not accept legal process (or any other requests for information) via email or fax, and will not respond to such requests.

Verified law enforcement entities can find additional information at the Roblox Law Enforcement Portal .
