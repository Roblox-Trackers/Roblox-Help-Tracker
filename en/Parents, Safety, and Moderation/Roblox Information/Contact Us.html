Contact Us

Roblox Customer Support team assists with Billing and Account issue related questions. Make sure to check our Roblox Help Articles before contacting us.

  • If you need further assistance, review the below information and fill out the Support form .
  • If you have questions about site features, experience performance, building or scripting, visit our Roblox Documentation and DevForum to learn more.
  • If you're having an issue joining an experience, using the Roblox platform, or playing an experience make sure to check out the Roblox Status page.

What information do we need when you contact us?

When you contact us, make sure to provide the following information below.

  • Roblox Username : Username that you need help with
    Note: If you need help with more than one username(s), add all of them in the description of the issue field while describing your issue.
  • What device are you having the problem on? : Select the device you need help with, where you made the purchase, etc
  • Type of help category : Select the topic for why you need help
