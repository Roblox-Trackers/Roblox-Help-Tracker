Where are my Robux?

Learn how to find your Robux balance in the mobile app , web browser , and Xbox as well as your Robux spending history. A Premium membership awards the stipend once every month.

  • If you receive a message about Robux being removed, see Message Received: Robux Removed from my Account .
  • If you have a Premium membership and looking for Premium stipends, see Robux Spending History .

Mobile App

Robux Balance

  1. Log in to the account where the Robux were purchased or earned.
  2. Tap the Robux icon at the top of the app.
  3. Your Robux balance is displayed at the top.

Web Browser

Robux Balance

  1. Log in to the account where the Robux were purchased or earned.
  2. Your Robux balance is shown in the upper right corner.

Robux Spending History

  1. Log in to the account where the Robux were purchased or earned.
  2. Click the Robux icon in the upper right corner.
  3. Click your Robux amount.
  4. The My Transactions page will load. You can change the different transaction types displayed by selecting the filter option in the upper left such as the ones listed below.
    • Summary : You can view your Robux incoming and outgoing summary.
    • Currency Purchases : You can view your Robux spending history.
    • Premium Stipends : You can view your Premium membership’s Robux allowance each month.
    • Purchases : You can view your purchase history for virtual items and passes.
    • Sales of Goods : You can view your sales history for virtual items and passes.
    • Engagement-Based Payouts : You can view the earned Robux based on the share of time that Premium members engage in your experiences.
    • Group Payouts : You can view the group pay contributors as a one-time payout or scheduling recurring payouts.

Xbox App

Robux Balance

  1. Open the Roblox app from the Xbox home menu.
  2. Log in to the account where the Robux were purchased or earned.
  3. Once you’re in the Roblox home menu, go to the Robux menu.
  4. You can see your Robux balance next to My Balance in the lower left corner.
