Roblox Buttons and Links Don't Work

If the buttons or links on the Roblox web site don't work for you ( they do not respond at all ) preventing you from being able to play or interact with the site/community, then the problem could be your browser's security settings.

While the information below is for changing these settings in Internet Explorer, you can visit your preferred web browser's website to find out how to modify its security settings. You will want to ensure that they are not set on high or that Roblox is within the browser's trusted site list.
  1. In Internet Explorer, click the Gear on the upper-right corner, and open the Internet Options .
  2. Go to the Security Tab.
  3. Look to see if your security is set to High .

If your security is set to High, lower your security setting or add Roblox to the Trusted Sites list. To do this:

  1. In Internet Explorer, click the Gear on the upper-right corner, and open the Internet Options .
  2. Go to the Security Tab
  3. Click the Trusted Sites check mark icon
  4. Click the Sites button
  5. Type the web address for Roblox ( http://www.roblox.com )
  6. Uncheck the Require box if it is checked
  7. Click the Add button
  8. Click the Close button
  9. Close all your browser Windows
  10. Open an Internet Explorer window and try again.
