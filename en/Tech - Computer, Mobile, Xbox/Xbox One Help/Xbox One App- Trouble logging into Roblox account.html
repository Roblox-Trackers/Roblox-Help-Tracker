Xbox One App: Trouble logging into Roblox account

To connect your Roblox account to your Xbox account, you will need to know your correct user name and password. If you have forgotten your password and need to change it, click here for information on how to reset your password.

Note: Please keep in mind that the Facebook connect feature is not available through Xbox, so you will need to login via your Roblox account password instead.

Disconnect Your Roblox and Xbox Accounts

    1. While on the Home screen, press the X button to access the Settings menu
    2. Next, select Account
    3. On this page, select UNLINK [YOUR GAMERTAG]

Alternatively, you can click here , go to the Security tab and under Xbox, click the Disconnect button.
