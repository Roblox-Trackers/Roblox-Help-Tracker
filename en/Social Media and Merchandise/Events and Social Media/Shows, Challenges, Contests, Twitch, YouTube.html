Shows, Challenges, Contests, Twitch, YouTube

There are many different ways to interact and have fun with Roblox. In addition to playing, building and hanging out with friends, you can also watch fantastic video content through our Twitch and YouTube channels!

Twitch Shows

Roblox works hard to produce fantastic live content for Twitch. On our channel you will find our fantastic weekly shows and even some special events! To join in the fun, click here .

Once there you can watch past episodes or, if we are currently streaming, you can watch and chat with us live! Whenever new content is broadcast, we will notify our users via a banner at the top of roblox.com.

What are the current shows and when do they happen?

  • Let's play - every Wednesday at 14:00 PT / 17:00 ET / 22:00 GMT
    In this show, our guests play exciting games.
  • The Creator Show - Every Thursday at 14:00 PT / 17:00 ET / 22:00 GMT
    If you've ever wanted to know the story behind creating your favorite Roblox games, this show is for you. In each episode our guests meet with the creators to hear their inspirations, methods and why they did certain things. So after answering the questions and telling the stories, it's time to play!
  • The Next Level - Every Friday at 15:00 PT / 18:00 ET / 23:00 GMT
    In this show, our guests show various Roblox games, interact with fans and announce new competitions / challenges in which the community can participate.

The Next Level : Twitch challenges

The next level , one of our live shows will often present challenges that users can participate in. Usually, these are done by streaming your gameplay on your Twitch channel and saving the movie as a highlight. To do this:

  1. Before you start streaming, make sure you've set your Twitch channel settings to automatically archive your broadcasts
  2. Stream your challenge game
  3. After finishing the goal, save the specific movie as a highlight. For help with this, click here
  4. Enter your username in the description of the highlight
  5. Send your voice to the requested presentation location

Twitter

To further explore our shows, there are some Twitter accounts to keep an eye on. Our official account, @Roblox , will provide you with a lot of news and behind-the-scenes Roblox information, including notices about shows, challenges and, in some cases, polls where you can vote on the contents of a future show!

If you missed an episode, but would still like to participate in any new challenge that may have been announced, the episodes are preserved and you can watch them after they air.
