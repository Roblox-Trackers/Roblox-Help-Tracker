Roblox on Social Media

Roblox has an active presence on several social media platforms. You can check all of these for official updates, community spotlights, sneak previews, and more!

YouTube - Here you can find building tutorials, trailers for upcoming events, and behind-the-scenes tours of Roblox’s headquarters. (https://www.youtube.com/user/roblox)

Official Twitter and Facebook - Follow our official channels for the latest info on all parts of the Roblox community.

  • Roblox News - For a spotlight on creators, players, and upcoming updates, follow our core social media accounts. (https://www.facebook.com/Roblox/) (https://twitter.com/Roblox) En Español: (https://twitter.com/RobloxEspanol)
  • Roblox Education - If you’re a teacher, student, or budding entrepreneur, Roblox Education offers free resources to get off the ground. (https://twitter.com/RobloxEdu)
  • Roblox Developer Relations - To get the scoop on technical updates and creator opportunities, follow our Developer Relations accounts.
    • English: (https://twitter.com/robloxdevrel)
    • Español: (https://twitter.com/robloxdevreles)
    • Português: (https://twitter.com/robloxdevrelptb)
    • Française: (https://twitter.com/robloxdevrelfr)
    • Deutsch: (https://twitter.com/robloxdevrelde)

Instagram - Check out our Instagram for gorgeous renders, illustrations by some of Roblox’s most popular artists, and previews of upcoming items. (https://www.instagram.com/roblox/?hl=en)
