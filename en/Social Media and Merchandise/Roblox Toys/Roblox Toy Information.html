Roblox Toy Information

Roblox has partnered with Jazwares to offer an awesome line of toys inspired by you, our citizens of Robloxia! The collection of toys is available from top retailers. Each toy includes a redeemable code to unlock exclusive virtual items.


Check out Jazwares’ website for information on toy availability, where to purchase toys, and their virtual items. Jazwares’ Collector’s Guide lists all the toys in a handy chart.

You can read news about Roblox Toys on our Blog .
