PlayStation App: Trouble logging into Roblox account

To connect your Roblox account to your PlayStation Network account on the app, you will need to know your Roblox username and password. If you have forgotten your password, and have trouble logging in, you will need to change it. Click here for information on how to reset your password.

Note: Keep in mind that the Facebook Connect feature is not available through PlayStation, so you will need to login via your Roblox account password instead.
