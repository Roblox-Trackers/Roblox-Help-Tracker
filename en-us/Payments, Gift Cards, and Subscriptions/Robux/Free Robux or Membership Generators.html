Free Robux or Membership Generators

Can I get free Robux, Memberships, or valuable items?

No. Any offer of free Robux, memberships, or valuable items is a scam. These are meant to trickÂ you into giving out your password or personal information, or make you click on a bad link. Their goal is to take your account and all your Robux and items.

There is no such thing as free Robux or membership offers, tricks, or codes. If a person, video, website, or game tries to tell you there is one, this is a scam and a violation of our Terms of Use . If you follow their advice and make false claims of a purchase in order to get free Robux or memberships, this is also a violation of our rules and could result in your account being deleted.

Best Practices:

  • Never enter your password anywhere other than the Roblox login page
  • Never share your password or sensitive information with another user
  • Don't click on suspicious offsite links

You can also read this Ways to Get Robux article about how to buy or earn Robux.

Make sure to always ask your parent/guardian to assist you with any purchase and never make a purchase without their permission. If you would like to ask your parent/guardian to make the purchase for you, you can let them know about the Roblox Premium membership page.

What should I do if I see scams?

Report messages, links, and experiences right away by using the Report Abuse links and buttons located around the apps and in every experience menu. Our moderators will be able to take correct action against people trying to scam your account. You will also be helping to make Roblox an even better place!

You can change who is allowed to send you messages, chats, and more in your Privacy Settings by following the steps in the Changing Privacy and Chat Settings article.
