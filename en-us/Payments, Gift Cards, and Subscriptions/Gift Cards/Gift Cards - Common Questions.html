Gift Cards - Common Questions

I can't find the Gift Card redemption page in the Roblox mobile app. Where can I redeem my Gift Card?

Roblox Gift Cards can only be redeemed on a web browser .

I can't find the Gift Card redemption page on my video game console. Where can I redeem my Gift card?

Roblox Gift Cards can only be redeemed on a web browser .

I am having an issue redeeming my Gift Card. What should I do?

Sometimes letters can look like numbers and numbers can look like letters. For example, the zero (0) in your Gift Card may actually be an “O”. Below are the common substitutions to try if you are having trouble redeeming your Gift Card.

0 (zero) - O

1 (one) - I (capital i)

2 (two) - Z

5 (five) - S

6 (six) - G / Q

8 (eight) - B

If you are still unable to redeem your Gift Card, please contact Roblox support .

How can I redeem a Gift Card that was purchased outside of my home country?

The currency of your Roblox Gift Card must match your account country.

I redeemed multiple Gift Cards but I only received one virtual item. What happened?

All Roblox Gift Cards come with a free virtual item. The item will be instantly granted to your account when you redeem your Gift Card. There are new items each month!

Note there is a limit of one unique virtual item per month. Visit roblox.com/giftcards to learn more.

Can I redeem multiple Gift Cards?

Yes! You can redeem as many Roblox Gift Cards to your account as you want. Gift Cards will need to be redeemed one at a time by visiting roblox.com/redeem .

Can I combine other payment types with Credit?

No. At this time it is not possible to combine or split payment types. If you do not have enough credit to pay for your entire purchase, you will be asked to use an alternate payment method.

Can I buy monthly memberships with Credit?

Yes! When choosing a payment method, select Roblox Credit if you've already redeemed the card. Or, you can select Redeem Roblox Card if you haven't yet redeemed the card.

Memberships purchased with Roblox Credit will automatically renew unless they are canceled before the renewal date. Your available Credit must cover the full price of your purchase at time of renewal.
