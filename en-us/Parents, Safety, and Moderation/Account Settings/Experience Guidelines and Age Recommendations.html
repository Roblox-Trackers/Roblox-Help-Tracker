Experience Guidelines and Age Recommendations

Roblox’s experience guidelines were created so all users can make informed decisions about the content they interact with. To determine the suitability of content for each age category, we examined global industry standards and consulted child development experts to guide our policy making.

When viewing an experience you can see the recommended age guideline as well as descriptors about what kind of content exists with an experience

All Ages

Content is generally suitable for all ages. May contain occasional mild violence and/or light unrealistic blood.

9+

Content is generally suitable for ages 9 and up. May contain repeated mild violence, heavy unrealistic blood, and/or mild crude humor.

13+

Content is generally suitable for ages 13 and up. May contain moderate violence, light realistic blood, moderate crude humor, and/or unplayable gambling content.

17+

Content is only suitable for ages 17 and up. May contain strong violence, heavy realistic blood, moderate crude humor, romantic themes, unplayable gambling content, the presence of alcohol, and/or strong language. These experiences are only available to 17+users who verified their ages by uploading a selfie and a photo of their government-issued ID.

Strong language will apply to both in-experience content and how users communicate within text and voice chat. Users must still comply with our Community Standards and Roblox’s 17+ Policies . Roblox won’t allow language that is used to harass, discriminate, incite violence, or threaten others, or used in a sexual context

Note: 17+ experiences are currently not accessible in Korea, Saudi Arabia, and Turkey
