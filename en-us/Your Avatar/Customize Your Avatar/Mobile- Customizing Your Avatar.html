Mobile: Customizing Your Avatar

Check out the Mobile: Viewing Your Inventory help article for how to access your inventory on mobile.

How to wear or remove an inventory item

Phone

  1. Select the item you wish to wear or remove from your inventory
  2. To wear the item, move the Wear This slide bar to Yes
  3. To remove the item, move the Wear This slide bar to No

Tablet

  1. Tap the More button at the bottom of the screen
  2. Tap Avatar
  3. From here you can dress your avatar as you would through the website. For assistance with this, please tap here .

How to change your avatar's skin color

Phone

  1. Log into your Roblox account on the Mobile Application.
  2. Go to the Avatar tab.
  3. Click on " Customize " and select " Body ".
  4. From the "Skin" tab, you'll be able to select the desired skin color.

Tablet

  1. Tap the More button at the bottom of the screen
  2. Tap Avatar
  3. From here you can change your skin color as you would through the website. For assistance with this, please tap here .
