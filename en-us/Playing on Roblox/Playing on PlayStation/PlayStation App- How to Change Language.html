PlayStation App: How to Change Language

We have multiple translations available for official Roblox text in the PlayStation App. The following languages are currently available:

  • Brazilian Portuguese
  • Chinese (Traditional and Simplified)
  • English
  • French
  • German
  • Indonesian
  • Japanese
  • Korean
  • Italian
  • Spanish
  • Thai

Note: In-experience content may still remain in English.

How to Change

Roblox on PlayStation does not currently support language changes. To change the language, this will need to be done via Roblox. Click here to learn more.
