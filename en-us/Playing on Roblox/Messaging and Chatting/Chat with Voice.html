Chat with Voice

What is Chat with Voice?

Chat with voice is a feature that allows people to communicate in Roblox as naturally as they communicate in the physical world.

Developers have the choice to integrate this technology in their experiences.

Chat with voice is currently available to all 13+ phone verified users in these countries: US, CA, UK, AU, NZ. Users not in these countries should use ID verification to enable chat with voice.

All 13+ users, who are phone verified , can opt-in to use this feature by visiting their Settings page. They will then be able to use chat with voice in any Roblox experience that supports it.

How do I enable Chat with Voice?

To enable the chat with voice, follow the instructions below:

  • Go to Account Settings
  • Select the Privacy Tab
  • Toggle the “Use microphone to chat with voice” selector to ON
    • The toggle will turn from gray to green, indicating that chat with voice has been enabled for your account.

13+ users are encouraged to enjoy this exciting way to communicate on Roblox, but must also continue to adhere to the Terms of Use and Community Standards as they would within any Roblox experience. Users that violate these rules may be suspended or removed from chat with voice and have further moderation actions taken against their Roblox account. Roblox also reserves the right to remove users from the chat with voice at any time at our discretion.

How can I tell who has Chat with Voice?

13+ users with chat with voice enabled will see a speaker icon next to the user name and above the avatar of other users that have voice chat enabled.

How to Mute, Block, or Report Abuse for Chat with Voice

Muting another user’s voice chat

You can mute another user in a chat with voice experience by clicking the speaker icon that will appear over their avatar’s head or next to their name in the experience menu. Muting only applies to chat with voice and not text chat and will only last while you are in that experience session.

Blocking another user

You can block another user in a chat with voice experience to prevent them from engaging with you in chat with voice or text chat. Please see our help article here: How to Block Another User .

Report Abuse

If someone is violating the Terms of Use or Community Standards , use the Report Abuse feature to file a report with our moderation team. You can now specify “Voice Chat” when using the Report Abuse feature. See our help article here: How to Report Rule Violations .


For more information on Roblox safety features, please see our help articles here: Roblox Safety .

How do I know which experiences support Chat with Voice?

To find out if an experience chat with voice, please review the experience details page that contains the “About” section. If you have enabled your device microphone for voice chat, within the “About” section, the “Voice Enabled” field will indicate if chat with voice is supported in that experience.

How can I turn off Chat with Voice?

To disable the chat with voice feature, follow the instructions below:

  • Go to Account Settings
  • Select the Privacy Tab
  • Toggle the “Use microphone to chat with voice” selector to OFF
    • The toggle will turn from green to gray, indicating that voice chat has been disabled for your account.
    • While you are not enabled for chat with voice you can toggle the selector back on at any time to re-enable chat with voice.
