The Creator Store

The Creator Store, formerly called the Creator Marketplace, is a collection of mostly free models, plugins, audio, fonts, images, meshes, and video that you can use to help create the experience of your dreams! Keep in mind that unlike clothing and gear, Creator Store items will not be able to be worn on your avatar.

Access the Creator Store

Via a Browser

  • Go to the Create page located in the top menu bar.
  • Click on the Store tab .

From Studio

Read the Creator Store documentation to learn how to access and use these freely available assets in your experiences.

Finding Creations

You can browse the offerings in different categories such as Models, Plugins, Audio, Fonts, Images, Meshes, and Video by clicking on the navigation menu or the three horizontal line menu on a mobile device. If you are looking for something particular, you can search by keyword or title using the Search box at the top of the page.

For models, you can filter by category. You can filter audio files by genre , vibes , or types of sound effects. If you are looking for any asset from a particular Creator, use the Creators filter on the left of the screen next to the name of the category (e.g. Browse Models).
