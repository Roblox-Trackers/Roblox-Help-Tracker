Creating and Selling Clothing

Roblox offers to customize your avatars. You can create your clothing and sell them on the Marketplace . To learn more about how to create clothing, see Classic Clothing .

  • To sell your clothing, you need to create classic clothing items first.
    • See How to sell items and Uploading for more details.
  • Clothing items can't be sold for free. See Classic Clothing fees .

Important Note:

  • All clothing must comply with the Roblox Community Standards .
  • Robux earned from selling clothes and other avatar items are held in pending status for up to 30 days being released for you to use.
  • This pending status is called escrow. When this holding period is over, the Robux will appear in your Robux total. You can check your Pending Robux by viewing My Transactions .
