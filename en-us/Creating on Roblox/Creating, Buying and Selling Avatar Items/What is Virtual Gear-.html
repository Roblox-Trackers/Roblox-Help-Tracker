What is Virtual Gear?

You can check out the large variety of Gear in the Avatar Shop to customize your avatar which are available for purchase with Robux. You can also see additional information about the specific gear on the Item Details Page .

Most of the Gears can’t be brought into Experiences. See Gear Restrictions on how to find it.

Gear Genres

Gear can be labeled as different genres to give you an idea of their type for your avatar or Experiences. Some examples are below.

  • Building
  • Horror
  • Town and City
  • Military
  • Comedy
  • Medieval
  • Adventure
  • Sci-Fi
  • Naval
  • FPS
  • RPG
  • Sports
  • Fighting
  • Western

Gear Restrictions

Most Experiences don’t allow Gears. To find out if gear is allowed, follow the steps below.

  1. Click Discover and you will see the variety of Experiences.
  2. Click the Experience that you like to find out if gear is allowed.
  3. Scroll down the Experience’s description page and find Allowed Gear .
  4. If you see the icon under Allowed Gear , the experience does not allow the use of outside gear.
    1. If you're not sure what the icon means, you can hover over the icon to see what kind of gear is allowed.
